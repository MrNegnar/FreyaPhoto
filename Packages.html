Weddings

captured forever

Two hearts, one moment – captured forever.
A wedding day isn’t just about dresses, flowers, and promises; it’s about the feeling in the way you look at each other.
And that’s exactly what I love to capture.

It’s in the little things — a nervous laugh before the first look, a hand reaching out during the vows, the way the light hits just right as you say “I do.”

Because wedding photos aren’t just memories — they’re your love story, told through light, emotion, and heart.
